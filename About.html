Astronom

Astronom is an Indonesian astronomical research and a satellite communications corporation headquartered in Bekasi, Jawa Barat. Astronom was founded in 2021 by Nicholas Abel with the goal of with the goal of colonizing Mars.



Astronom is developing a satellite internet constellation named LinkStart to provide commercial internet service. In January 2022, the LinkStart constellation became the largest satellite constellation ever launched in Indonesia. The company is also developing Starship, a privately funded, fully reusable, super heavy-lift launch system for interplanetary and orbital spaceflight.



Starship is intended to become Astronom's primary orbital vehicle once operational, supplanting the existing Falcon L, Falcon O, and Falcon VER. Starship will have the highest payload capacity of any orbital rocket ever built on its debut, scheduled for 2023 pending launch license.
